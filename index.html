ABOUT ME

Alex Vincent

My experience demonstrates 3+ years of skills in software engineering and UI/UX design. I consider myself a engineer with a designer's intuition; capable enough to deliver practical functionality to any organization while at the same time reimagining what software can be. From Sketch files to JavaScript frameworks, I understand what it takes to design and build applications across all platforms. Furthermore, my game design/engineering background grants me a unique perspective on what interactivity means for user experience.

As an individual, I consider myself to be a self-motivated individual with an passion to make a difference in the lives of my users. In short, I strive to simply think differently; to let my experiences, hard-work, and creativity be evident in all of my work.
